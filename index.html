Top Five Best Snacks to Find in Fort Lee

#1

Hiram's Hot Dog

When it comes to a hot dog none can beat Hiram's, if you want a quick filling snack this is the place. There is a reason its one on the list.

#2

Mochinut

Mochinut specializes in doughnuts with a unique shape. They come in all different flavors and are quite the treat.

#3

Q Tea Tapas' Bubble Tea

Bubble Tea is a unique tea, slush, or juice drink named for little pearls in the drink that bring a uniqe texture to the drink. Tea Tapas is a great place for Bubble Tea and other snacks such as crepes, poke bowls, rice triangles called onigiri.

#4

Jinn Rolled Ice-Cream

Rolled Ice-Cream is a uniqe take on ice-cream where you add different toppings to ice-cream rolled into logs and put in a cup.

#5

Oh k-Dog

At Oh K-dog is a place that specialized in Korean fried hot-dogs which is a hot dog fried in rice flour and covered with various toppings.
